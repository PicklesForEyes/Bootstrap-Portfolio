About Me

Benjamin Lawson is an aspiring web and/or software developer with a love for ice cream. This becomes problematic because Lawson is slightly, if not majorly, lactose intolerant. Nothing has stopped him though and he's not afraid to keep eating that scrumptious dairy product.

Lawson grew up in Durham, North Carolina and relocated to Greensboro, North Carolina for educational reasons. The college life was not correct for this young man as he dropped his music education schooling to attempt to reorganize his life.
